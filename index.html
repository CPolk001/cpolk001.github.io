Other Fun Knowledge

I grew up in Northern Virginia, born and raised in the same hometown that my parents met in high school (also attending said high school). Although I played little league baseball and later two years of junior varsity in high school, I spent more time on my bike exploring the neighborhood and the surrounding outdoors.


Typical boys my age growing up were interested in playing first-person mission games. Me? Not so much - I found enjoyment in simulators, playing games like Truck, Bus, and Ship Simulators. I also quickly developed my music skills, becoming a percussionist and later a tuba player. My music continues to evolve as I perform in annual summer musicals and teach music to middle and high school students.


I fell in love with the Shenadoah Valley as I've gotten older, though I've had family here in Woodstock for many years. My grandparents on my dad's side grew up and went to high school there, so my roots to the Valley span quite a few generations. My favorite part is the accessibility to West Virginia, as well as surrounding parks, hikes, and lakes. Even though my time at school is coming to an end, I hope to find myself as a resident here after graduation.
